FAQ

Common Questions

Is your healthcare AI HIPAA compliant?
Yes. We sign BAAs, encrypt all data in transit and at rest, and never store PHI unless contractually required. Our voice AI system is built on HIPAA-compliant infrastructure and trained to avoid capturing protected health information during calls.
Which EHR systems do you integrate with?
We integrate with Epic, Athena, eClinicalWorks, Dentrix, Eaglesoft, Open Dental, and most EHR/PM systems via API or HL7. If your system has an API, we can connect to it.
How long does it take to go live?
Most medical practices are live within 2-3 weeks. We handle EHR integration, call flow mapping, and voice AI training. You'll test the system before we go live on your main phone line.
Does the AI handle medical emergencies?
The AI is trained to identify emergency keywords (chest pain, shortness of breath, severe bleeding) and immediately escalate to your on-call line or direct patients to 911. We configure escalation rules during setup.
