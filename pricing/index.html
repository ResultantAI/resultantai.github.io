Frequently Asked Questions

What happens if I exceed my token limit?

Your requests automatically stop to prevent surprise bills. You'll get alerts at 80% and 90% usage. You can upgrade anytime or purchase additional tokens at overage rates.

Can I switch plans anytime?

Yes. Upgrade anytime and pay the prorated difference. Downgrade at your next billing cycle. Cancel in 2 clicks with no penalties.

Do tokens roll over?

No, tokens reset monthly. This keeps pricing simple and predictable. If you consistently use less, consider downgrading to save money.

What AI providers do you support?

OpenAI (GPT-4, GPT-4o, GPT-4o-mini), Anthropic (Claude Sonnet, Opus, Haiku), Google (Gemini Pro, Flash), Groq, Together AI, and more. One API key for all.

Is there a free trial?

Yes! The Free tier gives you 100K tokens/month forever, no credit card required. Test everything before upgrading to a paid plan.

How does smart routing work?

We automatically route requests to the most cost-effective provider based on your requirements. If a provider is down or rate-limited, we instantly failover to an alternative. You save money and never see downtime.
