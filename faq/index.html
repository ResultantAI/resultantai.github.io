// What We Build

ResultantAI builds AI-powered revenue systems for service businesses: AI voice receptionists that answer calls 24/7, dispatch and ticketing systems that replace paper workflows, and automation pipelines that connect your phone, CRM, and scheduling software.

We work primarily with HVAC, plumbing, roofing, electrical, landscaping, construction, propane, healthcare, and logistics businesses. If your company takes inbound calls and dispatches field teams, we have built something for your vertical.

An AI voice receptionist is a phone agent that uses AI to answer your inbound calls, speak naturally with callers, collect job details, qualify leads, book appointments, and route urgent calls to your team. It works 24/7 with no hold times, no missed calls, and no voicemail.

It is not a phone tree or a bot. It has a real conversation. Callers regularly do not know they are talking to AI until you tell them.

A traditional answering service uses human operators who read from a script and take messages. ResultantAI builds custom AI agents that understand your specific business, ask follow-up questions, qualify leads, push data directly into your CRM, and route jobs intelligently.

The AI is available 24/7 with no per-minute billing, no handoff delays, and no operator errors. It also costs a fraction of what a live answering service charges at scale.

Primary focus is blue-collar service businesses: HVAC, plumbing, roofing, electrical, landscaping, tree services, pool services, construction, propane delivery, and logistics/freight. We also work with healthcare practices, senior care facilities, staffing agencies, CPA firms, and B2B agencies.
