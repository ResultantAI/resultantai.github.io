## Design Tips
- Use Canva Pro or Figma for quick creation
- Keep text readable at thumbnail size
- High contrast (dark background, light text)
- Include ResultantAI branding
- Test preview at: https://www.opengraph.xyz/
- Optimize with TinyPNG before upload

## Quick Creation
1. Use Canva template: "LinkedIn Post" or "Facebook Post"
2. Resize to 1200x630px
3. Add text overlays with brand colors
4. Export as JPG (80-90% quality)
5. Upload to `/images/` folder
6. Test with Facebook Debugger: https://developers.facebook.com/tools/debug/
7. Test with Twitter Card Validator: https://cards-dev.twitter.com/validator
