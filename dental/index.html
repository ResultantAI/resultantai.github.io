Frequently Asked Questions

Is this HIPAA compliant?

Yes. All call recordings are encrypted at rest and in transit. We sign a BAA (Business Associate Agreement) before deployment. No PHI is stored beyond what's necessary for appointment booking.

Will patients know they're talking to AI?

Most don't, but we're transparent if asked. Our voices are natural and conversational, not robotic. You can listen to real call samples before deciding.

What practice management systems do you support?

Dentrix, Eaglesoft, Open Dental, and Curve Dental natively. For other systems, we integrate via API or a middle-layer connector. We'll confirm compatibility during your audit.

Can the AI handle complex calls like treatment plan questions?

For clinical questions, the AI collects the caller's info and routes to the right team member with a full summary. It handles booking, insurance basics, and scheduling — complex clinical questions always go to your team.
