What We Automate

Turn 30-40 hours/week of admin chaos into 5 hours of oversight.

Intelligent Work Order Routing

Tenant submits maintenance request → AI triages urgency → Matches to pre-approved vendor by trade & availability → Auto-schedules with tenant availability → Updates all parties → Tracks completion.

Time Saved: 35 min per ticket → 5 min review. 7.5 hours/week on 15 tickets.

Vacancy Turnover Orchestration

Move-out notice → Auto-schedules inspection → Books cleaner, painter, repairs → Coordinates photographer → Posts to listing sites → Schedules showings → Sends apps to qualified leads → Lease execution.

Impact: 21-day average → 14 days. Save $350-1,050 per unit (7 days × $50-150).

Tenant Communication Autopilot

Rent reminders (5 days before due, day-of, day-after). Late payment escalation sequence. Maintenance status updates. Lease renewal reminders (90/60/30 days). FAQ auto-responses via SMS/email/portal.

Time Saved: 50 messages/week × 3 min = 2.5 hours → 15 min review.

After-Hours Emergency Triage

AI voice agent handles after-hours calls. Assesses if true emergency (water, heat, lockout). Routes emergencies to on-call vendor + notifies manager. Logs non-emergencies for next business day. Tenants get instant acknowledgment.

Impact: 80% of after-hours calls handled without waking manager.

Vendor Performance Tracking

Auto-logs vendor response times, completion rates, tenant feedback. Flags slow vendors. Generates quarterly scorecards. Recommends vendor mix optimization based on performance + cost data.

Impact: Reduce maintenance costs 10-15% by identifying underperformers.

Proactive Lease Renewal Campaign

Automated renewal outreach at 90/60/30 days before lease end. Market rate analysis for renewal pricing. Personalized renewal offers. Tracks responses, schedules signing, sends executed leases to accounting.

Impact: 15-20% higher renewal rate = fewer vacancies, lower turnover costs.
