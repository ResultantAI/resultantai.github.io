The Problem

The Missed Call Crisis

Wayne Conn Plumbing is a mid-sized plumbing company serving Jacksonville, FL, handling over 200 service calls per month. Their services include emergency repairs, installations, and regular maintenance for residential and commercial customers.

But they had a revenue leak:

  • 18 missed calls every week — customers hanging up and calling the next plumber
  • 20+ minute response times during business hours
  • After-hours calls went unanswered — leaving emergency customers frustrated
  • No call tracking system — couldn't measure what was being lost
  • Lost revenue and damaged client relationships from slow follow-up

In the plumbing business, speed wins. The first company to answer gets the job. Wayne Conn was losing jobs every single day because customers couldn't reach them.

The Solution

24/7 AI Receptionist That Never Misses a Call

We built an AI-powered phone system that answers every call instantly, qualifies the customer, schedules the appointment, and routes urgent calls to the on-call technician — all without human intervention.

System Architecture:

1. AI Receptionist (Retell AI)

Answers every call in under 3 seconds. Sounds natural, follows call scripts perfectly, handles objections, and books appointments directly into the calendar.

2. Smart Dashboard & Call Tracking

Real-time dashboard shows all incoming calls, call dispositions, booking status, and revenue attribution. Management can monitor service calls and performance live.

3. Booking System Integration

Seamless integration with Wayne Conn's existing booking software. AI checks availability and schedules appointments instantly — no back-and-forth.

4. Emergency Routing Logic

Detects urgent keywords (flooding, burst pipe, no water) and immediately routes to on-call technician via SMS and phone notification.

Key Technical Features:

  • Sub-10-second pickup time: Customers never wait on hold.
  • Natural conversation flow: AI understands plumbing terminology, handles objections, and upsells service plans.
  • 100% call capture: Every call is logged, transcribed, and tracked.
  • After-hours coverage: Works nights, weekends, holidays — no extra staffing costs.
  • CRM integration: Customer data flows directly into existing systems.
