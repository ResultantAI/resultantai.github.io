AI Gateway vs Portkey: Which is Better?

Bottom line: AI Gateway is better for predictable pricing (budget-based with intelligent routing). Portkey is better for enterprise teams needing advanced observability. With AI Gateway's $5,000 monthly budget and smart routing to cheaper models, you get significantly more value than Portkey's pay-per-token model.

Pricing Comparison

AI Gateway Pricing

AI Gateway uses budget-based pricing: $99/month includes a $5,000 usage budget. Combined with intelligent routing that automatically uses cheaper models (Gemini Flash at $0.10/M vs GPT-4 at $30/M), your budget goes much further. Budget caps prevent bill shock-predictable costs, no surprises.

Portkey Pricing

Portkey uses a platform-fee model: $49/month platform subscription, then you pay OpenAI/Anthropic/Google separately for all tokens consumed. No budget caps means potential for bill shock. No intelligent routing means you pay full price for every request, even simple ones.

Which Should You Choose?

Choose AI Gateway if:

✓ You use 2-4M tokens/month consistently
✓ You want predictable, all-inclusive pricing
✓ You prioritize simplicity over advanced features
✓ You're an agency billing clients and need clean invoicing
✓ You want to prevent bill shock with hard spending caps

Choose Portkey if:

✓ You use 10M+ tokens/month (lower per-token cost)
✓ You need advanced observability and analytics
✓ You want prompt versioning and A/B testing
✓ You have an enterprise team that needs collaboration features
✓ You're okay managing separate platform + provider billing

Can You Use Both?

No-both are LLM proxy layers, so you can't route through AI Gateway AND Portkey simultaneously. You must choose one. However, you could use AI Gateway for production traffic and Portkey for development/testing if needed.
