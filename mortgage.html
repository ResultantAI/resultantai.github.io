The Processing Bottlenecks

Document Collection Hell

30-50 documents per loan. Pay stubs, tax returns, bank statements, employment verification. Average 7-12 follow-ups per borrower. Loan officers spend 25-35 hours per loan chasing paperwork instead of originating.

Income Verification Delays

7-14 day delays waiting for employment/income verification. Manual calls to employers, tax transcript requests to IRS, VOE forms. 40% of loans stall here. Borrowers lose rate locks, deals fall through.

Compliance Documentation Chaos

200+ TRID/RESPA/HMDA compliance checks per loan. Manual review of disclosures, timelines, fee accuracy. 30-40% of delays caused by compliance errors caught late. Fines, rework, lost deals.

Borrower Status Update Overload

15-25 borrower touch points per loan. "Where's my loan?" calls, status emails, milestone updates. Loan officers spend 8-12 hours per loan on borrower communication that adds zero value to closing.

Appraisal & Title Coordination

5-10 phone calls/emails coordinating appraisers, title companies, inspectors. Scheduling conflicts, report delays, re-ordering when borrowers don't respond. 3-7 day delays on 30% of loans.

Pipeline Visibility Black Hole

Manual pipeline tracking in spreadsheets or outdated LOS. Can't see where loans are stuck until too late. Processors, underwriters, closers working in silos. 25% of pipeline delays invisible to management.

What We Automate

Turn 45-60 day closing timelines into 25-30 days with 50% less manual work.

Intelligent Document Collection

Borrower applies → System auto-requests docs based on loan type → SMS/email reminders with secure upload portal → AI validates completeness & readability → Auto-extracts key data → Flags discrepancies → Notifies loan officer only when intervention needed.

Time Saved: 25 hours → 8 hours per loan. Follow-ups drop from 7-12 → 2-3.

Instant Income & Employment Verification

Integrates with The Work Number, IRS Income Verification Express Service (IVES), and bank account aggregators. Pulls pay data, tax transcripts, deposit history automatically. Manual VOE only when automated sources fail (15% of cases vs 100%).

Impact: 7-14 day delay → 1-2 hours. 85% of verifications instant.

Automated Compliance Monitoring

Real-time TRID/RESPA timeline tracking. Auto-validates fee accuracy, disclosure delivery dates, 3-day waiting periods. Flags compliance risks before submission. Generates audit trail automatically. Pre-populates HMDA data from application.

Impact: 30-40% error rate → 5-8%. Reduce compliance delays from days to hours.

Borrower Communication Autopilot

Milestone-based status updates (application received, docs collected, underwriting, clear to close). Automated doc reminders with deadlines. Rate lock expiration alerts. Personalized closing countdown timeline. AI chatbot answers FAQ 24/7.

Time Saved: 8-12 hours per loan → 1-2 hours. Borrower satisfaction +35%.

Third-Party Coordination Hub

Auto-orders appraisal when conditions met. Schedules with borrower availability. Tracks title, inspection, HOA cert timelines. Sends reminders to vendors. Escalates delays automatically. Centralized status dashboard for all parties.

Impact: Reduce third-party delays from 30% → 10% of loans.

Pipeline Intelligence Dashboard

Real-time loan stage tracking with bottleneck alerts. Predicts which loans will miss close dates 7-10 days early. Shows processor/underwriter workload balance. Identifies where loans are stuck (docs, verification, appraisal, etc). Auto-routes to fix blockers.

Impact: 25% of delays become visible → 90%. Proactive intervention vs reactive firefighting.
