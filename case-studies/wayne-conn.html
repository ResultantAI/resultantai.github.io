The Challenge

Wayne Conn Plumbing, a family-owned business serving the greater Baltimore area, had a problem that's common in trades: missed calls = missed revenue.

The reality:

  • 40% of calls came after hours or during jobs
  • Voicemail callback rate was only 35%
  • Wayne estimated losing $10K+ per month in missed opportunities
  • Hiring a full-time receptionist would cost $3K+ monthly with limited hours

"I'd be up on a ladder and my phone would ring. By the time I got down, they'd already called my competitor. Those lost jobs add up fast." — Wayne Conn

The Solution

ResultantAI built a custom AI voice agent that handles incoming calls like a trained receptionist:

What the AI Does

  • Answers every call within 2 rings, 24/7/365
  • Qualifies emergencies vs. routine appointments
  • Books appointments directly into Wayne's calendar
  • Captures job details (issue type, property info, urgency)
  • Sends SMS confirmations to customers automatically
  • Routes true emergencies to Wayne's cell

Technical Details

The system was built using:

  • Vapi.ai for AI voice infrastructure
  • Claude AI for natural conversation and decision-making
  • Google Calendar API for scheduling
  • Twilio for SMS notifications

Total build time: 2 weeks from initial consultation to live deployment.

"My phone bill went up $50/month, but my revenue went up $5,000/month. The AI never sleeps, never takes a vacation, and never forgets to book an appointment. Best investment I've made in my business."

The Results

First 90 days:

  • +$5,200 average monthly revenue (confirmed new bookings)
  • 127 appointments booked that would have been missed calls
  • 94% answer rate (up from 60% with manual answering)
  • 4.8/5 customer satisfaction rating from post-call surveys
  • Zero missed emergency calls

Unexpected Benefits

Beyond the revenue numbers, Wayne discovered several unexpected advantages:

  • Better work-life balance: No more interrupting dinner to answer calls
  • Professional image: Customers assume he's running a larger operation
  • Data insights: The system tracks peak call times, common issues, and seasonal trends
  • Customer education: AI provides accurate pricing estimates and service timelines

Key Takeaways

  1. Small businesses win big with AI: You don't need enterprise budgets to automate
  2. Voice AI is remarkably good now: Customers often don't realize they're talking to AI
  3. ROI is immediate: Wayne recovered costs in week 3, everything after is pure profit
  4. Scalability matters: The system costs the same whether it handles 10 or 1,000 calls
