The Challenge

Honcho, a fast-growing B2B SaaS company, was drowning in inbound leads. Their sales team spent 60% of their time qualifying leads that weren't ready to buy, leaving little time for actual selling.

Pain points:

  • Manual lead qualification took 15-20 minutes per lead
  • 70% of qualified leads turned out to be poor fits
  • Sales team morale was low due to wasted time
  • Opportunities were falling through the cracks

The Solution

ResultantAI built an intelligent lead qualification system that automatically:

  • Enriched leads with company data (revenue, employee count, tech stack)
  • Scored leads based on fit criteria using AI analysis
  • Routed opportunities to the right sales reps based on territory and expertise
  • Generated personalized talking points for each qualified lead

Technical Implementation

The system was built using:

  • Make.com for workflow automation
  • Claude AI for lead analysis and scoring
  • Clearbit API for company enrichment
  • HubSpot CRM integration

Total build time: 4 weeks from kickoff to production deployment.

"We went from spending hours manually qualifying leads to having AI do it in seconds. Our sales team is now focused on closing deals, not chasing dead ends. The $382K in pipeline speaks for itself."
— Sarah Chen, VP of Sales at Honcho

The Results

After 90 days:

  • $382K in qualified pipeline generated
  • 87% reduction in time spent on lead qualification
  • 3.2X increase in conversion rate (qualified lead → opportunity)
  • 23% faster time from lead to first meeting
  • 97% time saved on manual data entry and research

Beyond the Numbers

The sales team reported dramatically improved morale. Instead of wasting time on unqualified leads, they were having meaningful conversations with prospects who were ready to buy.

The system also uncovered patterns in high-value leads that the team hadn't noticed before, leading to refined targeting and messaging strategies.

Key Takeaways

  1. Speed matters: 4-week build time meant Honcho saw ROI in Q1 instead of Q3
  2. AI excels at pattern recognition: The system identified ideal customer profiles better than manual scoring
  3. Automation frees up humans: Sales reps focused on relationships, not data entry
  4. Incremental improvements compound: Small efficiency gains led to massive pipeline growth
