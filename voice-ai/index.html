FAQ

Common questions

How long does it take to go live? +

Typically 2-3 weeks from kickoff to first live call. We handle everything: script writing, voice selection, CRM integration, calendar connection, and testing. You just need to do a 1-hour kickoff call and one round of review.

Does it sound like a robot? +

No. We use natural-sounding voices and conversational scripts that don't feel scripted. Most callers don't realize they're talking to AI. You can listen to real call recordings on our demo page before committing.

What happens when the AI can't handle a call? +

The agent does a warm transfer to a live team member and passes along a full call summary so the customer doesn't have to repeat themselves. You control the transfer rules - by call type, time of day, or custom triggers.

What integrations do you support? +

HubSpot, Salesforce, ServiceTitan, Google Calendar, Calendly, Acuity, and most calendar or CRM systems via API. If you use something else, we can typically integrate via webhook or Zapier.

What does it cost? +

Setup is a one-time project fee ($2,500 - $5,000 depending on complexity). Monthly platform and maintenance is $500 - $1,500/mo depending on call volume. Most clients see ROI within 30 days.

Can I see it before I commit? +

Yes. Book a discovery call and we'll show you a live demo built for your industry. You can also listen to existing deployments on our demo page. We don't charge anything until you've approved the scope and agreed to move forward.
