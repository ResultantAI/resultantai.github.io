What Is an AI Voice Receptionist?

A phone agent powered by AI that handles your inbound calls the way a trained dispatcher would - without the salary, training time, or sick days.

Answers Every Call

Picks up in 3 seconds. No hold music. No voicemail. The caller hears a natural voice that sounds like a real person, because it is trained to be one.

Qualifies and Captures

Asks the right questions. Gets the address, job type, urgency level, and contact info. Every call becomes a structured lead - not a missed voicemail.

Routes Instantly

Urgent jobs go to your on-call tech by text or call transfer. Non-urgent jobs land in your CRM for morning dispatch. Nothing falls through.

Common Questions

What does an AI voice receptionist actually sound like?

Natural and conversational. Not robotic. The voice is trained to match your business tone - professional, friendly, or technical depending on your vertical. Book a demo and we'll call you with it live.

Can it handle complex calls, not just simple inquiries?

Yes. It handles multi-step conversations: collecting job details, asking follow-up questions, explaining pricing ranges, confirming availability, and escalating when it hits something it cannot resolve. Complex cases get routed to a human with a full summary of the call.

What happens when a caller wants to talk to a person?

The agent transfers the call to whoever you designate - your office line, an on-call tech, or a manager. You define the escalation rules during setup.

What does it cost?

Setup starts at $1,500 for a single-line deployment with standard CRM integration. Most builds are $2,500-$4,500 depending on complexity. Monthly support is $500-$1,200/month. Every engagement pays back within 18 days on average.

Do I need to replace my current phone system?

No. We typically set up call forwarding from your existing number, or provision a new number that rings the AI first and transfers to your team when needed. No hardware changes required.
